PROJECT 04 Web App + Automation

Secure Invoice Management

A full-stack secure web application with n8n backend for complete invoice lifecycle management — from creation to MyDATA submission.

About the Project

DynaWorks Invoice is a comprehensive invoice management system built for businesses operating in Greece. It handles the entire invoice lifecycle from creation through submission to the Greek tax authority's MyDATA system.

Key features include:

  • Secure authentication with role-based access control
  • Invoice creation with automatic calculations and Greek VAT handling
  • Client and product database management
  • Automatic MyDATA XML generation and submission
  • Real-time status tracking and submission confirmations
  • PDF generation with professional invoice templates
  • Dashboard with analytics and reporting

The Challenge

Greek businesses are required to submit invoices to the MyDATA system, but the process is complex and error-prone. Manual submission takes time, and mistakes can result in penalties. The client needed a streamlined system that handles compliance automatically while providing a modern, user-friendly interface.

Results

The system now processes all invoices automatically with 100% MyDATA compliance. Invoice creation time dropped from 15 minutes to under 2 minutes. The client has complete visibility into their invoicing with real-time dashboards and reporting.
