PROJECT 03 AI Automation

Personal AI Email Agent

End-to-end automation that transforms email from a daily task into a fully automated workflow with intelligent categorization, prioritization, and response drafting.

About the Project

This AI-powered email agent automates the entire email management workflow. It monitors incoming emails, intelligently categorizes them, prioritizes based on urgency and importance, and even drafts contextual responses for review.

The system includes:

  • Real-time email monitoring via Gmail API integration
  • AI-powered categorization (work, personal, newsletters, spam, urgent)
  • Smart prioritization based on sender history, content analysis, and deadlines
  • Automatic response drafting for common email types
  • Daily digest summaries with action items highlighted
  • Calendar integration for scheduling-related emails

The Challenge

The client was spending 2-3 hours daily managing email — reading, sorting, responding, and following up. Important emails were getting buried, and response times were suffering. The goal was to reduce email management time to under 30 minutes while improving response quality and speed.

Results

Email management time dropped from 3 hours to 25 minutes daily. Response times improved by 60%, and the client reports feeling more in control of their inbox. The AI drafts are accurate enough that 70% need only minor edits before sending.
